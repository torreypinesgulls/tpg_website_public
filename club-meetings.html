Club Meetings

The Torrey Pines Gulls hosts bi-monthly meetings via zoom throughout the year. These meetings began in 2021 out of the need to continue meeting as a club during the Covid-19 pandemic. However, they have continued on, helping to bring the club together. Each meeting usually reviews the upcoming calendar of events and features a speaker on an interesting topic of interest to RC soaring or flight in general. Meetings are open to the public but club membership is encouraged.

The Torrey Pines Gulls (TPG) is a world-renowned club of 100+ passionate individuals dedicated to the thrill of remote-control soaring, from casual flying to high-level competition. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, TPG offers exciting opportunities to fly a variety of RC sailplanes - from nearly indestructible foam trainers to sleek, high-performance composite models. New members enjoy hands-on training with expert instructors, ensuring a fun and safe learning experience. With spectacular flying sites like the Torrey Pines Gliderport and a packed contest schedule, TPG is the perfect place to soar, and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Join us for an event and discover the excitement of R/C soaring!
